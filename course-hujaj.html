Pilgrim Track

Living Arabic for Hujaj

Equip yourself for the journey of a lifetime. A specialized, practical track designed to help you navigate Hajj and Umrah with confidence, independence, and peace of mind.

Course Overview

Performing Hajj or Umrah is a profoundly physical and spiritual undertaking. Language barriers should not add to the exhaustion or prevent you from fully experiencing the holy cities. This short-form, high-impact track bypasses complex grammar to focus strictly on survival and transactional Arabic. You will learn the exact phrases needed to navigate airports, interact with authorities, request assistance, and fulfill your Manasik (rituals) seamlessly.

What You Will Master

Ritual Terminology

Understand the specific terms used during Tawaf, Sa'i, and standing at Arafat, allowing you to focus on worship rather than confusion.

Travel & Logistics

Confidently ask for directions, coordinate with taxi drivers, and manage hotel accommodations without relying on a translator.

Shopping & Dining

Navigate local marketplaces, negotiate prices politely, and order meals with ease in the bustling streets of Makkah and Madinah.

Health & Safety

Learn crucial, life-saving phrases to communicate effectively with medical staff, security personnel, or guides in an emergency.

Target Audience

  • Muslims preparing for their upcoming Hajj or Umrah journey.
  • Tour operators and group guides who manage pilgrim delegations in Saudi Arabia.
  • Families equipping elderly parents with the language tools to stay safe while traveling.
