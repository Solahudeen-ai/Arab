Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: My flight leaves in 2 weeks. Is it too late to enroll?
We offer an accelerated "Crash Course" track specifically for late enrollees. You will focus exclusively on the core survival phrases and download the cheat sheets for offline use on the plane.
Q2: My spouse and I are traveling together. Do we need two subscriptions?
We offer special family discounts for couples traveling together. The Signature tier includes a family prep guide, and you can share the portal access. However, for the Elite on-ground support hotline, both travelers must register if they want independent phone access.
Q3: Can I keep the materials for Umrah next year?
Yes! All tiers grant lifetime access to the digital course materials and downloadable cheat sheets, so you can refresh your memory before any future trips to Saudi Arabia.
Q4: How exactly does the Elite On-Ground Support Hotline work?
When you arrive in Saudi Arabia, you will be given a dedicated WhatsApp number. If you face a language barrier (e.g., at a pharmacy or hotel desk), you can send a voice note or initiate a call during operational hours, and our instructor will translate your needs in real-time.
