Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: Is the focus strictly on Classical (Fusha) Arabic?
Yes. This curriculum is designed exclusively around Quranic and classical Fusha Arabic, ensuring your grammar and vocabulary are appropriate for formal religious leadership and scholarly discourse.
Q2: Will this schedule conflict with my Jumu'ah preparation?
Not at all. The program is fully modular. You access the core materials on your own time—whether that's after Fajr or late at night. Coaching sessions are scheduled around your community obligations.
Q3: Do I receive a recognized certification?
Yes. Upon successful completion of the Scholar or Elite tracks, you will receive an official certification backed by our academic board, validating your advanced comprehension and rhetorical delivery.
Q4: How does the pronunciation feedback work privately?
You will submit private voice notes of your recitation or Khutbah excerpts via our secure portal. Our senior scholars will reply directly to you with specific, discreet corrections regarding Tajweed, syntax, and delivery.
Q5: Can I pay in installments?
The pricing listed is for one-time payments for full access. However, if you require a split payment arrangement, please contact our administrative desk to discuss custom accommodation.
