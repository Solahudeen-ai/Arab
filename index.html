Dr. Salahudeen Murtada Adekunle is a highly accomplished scholar bringing profound expertise to our fully online-based learning community. His academic foundation spans multiple prestigious institutions, including the University of Kuwait and the University of Ibadan.

With a teaching career originating in 1973, he serves as a Senior Lecturer at Emmanuel Alayande University of Education. He is a prolific author of foundational texts such as A Compendium of Tajwiid and has served as Chairman of NATAIS (Oyo State).
