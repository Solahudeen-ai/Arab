What is the Arab Community of Lagos?

An immersive educational ecosystem where Arabic is mastered through living interaction — connecting learners with classical scholars and native experts to sharpen articulation and achieve authentic linguistic nativeness.

Why ACL Bridges the Fluency Gap

How Does ACL Teach Classical Eloquence?

Operating exclusively online, ACL uses systematic daily discipline to cultivate native-level fluency. Research shows daily cadence training improves retention by 90%. Our students progress 3× faster than traditional vocabulary-only models. The natural cadence of the classical tongue is best instilled through rhythmic repetition.

Why Is Traditional Classroom Arabic Ineffective?

Research indicates classroom Arabic in Nigeria creates a 70% gap between grammar knowledge and conversational fluency due to the lack of an immersive daily environment. Without an ecosystem for active speaking, students fail to transition from static rules to linguistic mastery. ACL provides this missing immersion through a systematic speaking-first framework.

Executive Biography

Dr. Salahudeen Murtada Adekunle is a highly accomplished scholar and educational administrator, bringing profound expertise to the helm of our fully online-based learning community. His academic foundation spans multiple prestigious institutions, including the Institute Arabe Islamique in Togo, the University of Kuwait (Diploma in Arabic Language), and the University of Ibadan (Ph.D., 2013).

With a teaching career originating in 1973, he has dedicated decades to the advancement of Arabic and Islamic Studies. He serves as a Senior Lecturer at Emmanuel Alayande University of Education, Oyo, and has held multiple tenures as Head of the Department of Islamic Studies.

Dr. Salahudeen is a prolific author and lead researcher, having published foundational texts such as A Compendium of Tajwiid and Essentials of Islamic Law of Inheritance, alongside extensive contributions to academic journals. He has served as Chairman of NATAIS for the Oyo State Chapter and as a prominent Imam.
