Common Inquiries

Are the Academy courses fully online?
Yes. The Arab Community of Lagos operates strictly as an online-based institution to remove geographical barriers and provide flexible learning across Nigeria and beyond.
How do you verify teachers for schools?
We conduct a rigorous multi-step vetting process including TRCN certification checks, background screening, and a mandatory legal guarantor framework to ensure total compliance with employment laws.
Is there a fee for schools to use the portal?
We operate on a standard recruitment agency commission structure (typically a percentage of the first-year salary) or a retainer model, depending on the volume of hiring required.
