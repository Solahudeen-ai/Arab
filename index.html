Executive Biography

Dr. Salahudeen Murtada Adekunle is a highly accomplished scholar and educational administrator, bringing profound expertise to the helm of our fully online-based learning community. His academic foundation spans multiple prestigious institutions, including the Institute Arabe Islamique in Togo, the University of Kuwait where he earned a Diploma in Arabic Language, and the University of Ibadan, where he was awarded his Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) in 2013.

With a teaching career originating in 1973, he has dedicated decades to the advancement of Arabic and Islamic Studies. He serves as a Senior Lecturer at Emmanuel Alayande University of Education, Oyo. His administrative excellence is consistently demonstrated by his multiple tenures as the Head of the Department of Islamic Studies at the same institution.

Dr. Salahudeen is a prolific author and lead researcher. He has published foundational texts such as A Compendium of Tajwiid: (Quranic Rules of Recitation) and Essentials of Islamic Law of Inheritance, alongside extensive contributions to academic journals. His leadership extends deeply into the community, where he has served as the Chairman of the Nigeria Association of Teachers of Arabic and Islamic Studies (NATAIS) for the Oyo State Chapter and as a prominent Imam.

Our Story

Operating exclusively online, our mission is to cultivate the precise articulation and persuasive rhythm of a native speaker through systematic, daily discipline. We move students beyond basic vocabulary, instilling the natural cadence and eloquence of the classical tongue.

The Educational Divide

Research shows a critical gap between the Arabic taught in classrooms and actual conversational fluency of most speakers. For our students in Nigeria, the daily enviroment simply does not provide the immersion needed to practice and thrive. This observation is backed by science: research shows that without a supportive ecosystem for consistent, active speaking, achieving true linguistic mastery is nearly impossible. That is exactly why this community was built
