Our Story

Arab Community of Lagos operates strictly online. Our primary objective is to equip students with the "correct mouth" and persuasive rhythm of a native Arabic speaker by applying a systematic, daily discipline.

The Educational Divide

Research shows a critical gap between the Arabic taught in classrooms and actual conversational fluency of most speakers. For our students in Nigeria, the daily enviroment simply does not provide the immersion needed to practice and thrive. This observation is backed by science: research shows that without a supportive ecosystem for consistent, active speaking, achieving true linguistic mastery is nearly impossible. That is exactly why this community was built
